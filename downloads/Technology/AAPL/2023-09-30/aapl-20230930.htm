Item 1. Business

Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories worldwide. The Company also sells various related services.

In fiscal 2023, the Company generated net sales of $394.3 billion and net income of $97.0 billion. The Company's financial condition remains strong, with $166.3 billion in cash and marketable securities.

Item 1A. Risk Factors

Risks related to the Company's business and industry include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Global and regional economic conditions
  • Intense competition in the markets for the Company's products and services
  • Dependence on manufacturing and logistics services provided by third parties
  • Dependence on the availability of certain components and services
